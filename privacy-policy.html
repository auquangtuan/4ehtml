Privacy Policy

At 4E Kids, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information we collect from children aged 2 to 7 years old who use our early education app ("App"). Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your child's information.

1. Information We Collect

  • 1.1 Personal Information: We do not collect personal information from children without the consent of their parent or guardian. If you are a parent or guardian and provide personal information about your child, you are giving us permission to collect, use, and disclose that information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
  • 1.2 Non-Personal Information: We may collect non-personal information automatically when your child uses our App. This may include device information, such as the type of device used, device identifiers, operating system, and IP address. We may also collect information about the App usage, such as the activities accessed and the time spent on each activity.

2. Use of Information

  • 2.1 We may use the information we collect to provide and improve our App, personalize the user experience, and understand how the App is being used.
  • 2.2 We do not use personal information for marketing purposes without obtaining prior parental consent.
  • 2.3 Non-personal information may be used for analytical purposes, including analyzing usage trends and improving the App's features and functionality.

3. Disclosure of Information

  • 3.1 We do not disclose personal information of children to third parties unless required by law or as necessary to provide the services requested by the child's parent or guardian.
  • 3.2 We may disclose non-personal information to third-party service providers who assist us in operating and improving the App. These service providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of the information and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

4. Data Security

  • 4.1 We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
  • 4.2 However, please note that no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your child's information.

5. Parental Rights and Choices

  • 5.1 As a parent or guardian, you have the right to review and delete any personal information we have collected about your child. You can also refuse to allow further collection or use of your child's information by contacting us using the information provided below.
  • 5.2 You can also request to access or correct non-personal information by contacting us.

6. Updates to this Privacy Policy

  • 6.1 We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website or through the App.
